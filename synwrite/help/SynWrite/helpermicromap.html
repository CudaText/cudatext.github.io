Micro-Map

Micro-Map is a vertical colored bar near editor's right border. To show it, turn on "Show micro-map bar" flag in the Options dialog.
It has functionality similar to vertical scroll-bar, but with additional coloring applied. You can click micro-map to scroll the document. While mouse cursor is over micro-map, statusbar shows hint with editor line number.

Bar shows the following colored labels:

  • Full-width bar (light blue) - current editor view area
  • Marks along left border - states of editor lines:
    • new line (green)
    • modified line (yellow)
    • saved line (dark blue)
  • Marks along right border:
    • search marks (green)
    • misspelled words marks (red)

Notes:

  • Search marks appear after "Find all" command performed, or after option "Highlight all occurences of selected word" has found some matches.
  • Misspelled words marks appear after the "Live spelling" feature activates.
